Privacy Policy for Saba Chat

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Saba Chat ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, display name, profile photo
  • Profile Data: Country, gender, profile picture (stored as base64 data)
  • Authentication: Google Sign-In credentials (email and basic profile information)

2.2 Usage and Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature interactions, session duration
  • Performance Data: App crashes, error logs, performance metrics

2.3 Communication Data

  • Chat Messages: Text messages sent in chat rooms
  • Voice Calls: Audio data during voice calls (processed in real-time, not stored)
  • Music Files: Locally stored music files for room playback
  • Gift Transactions: Virtual gift exchanges and coin transfers

2.4 Financial Information

  • Virtual Currency: Coin balance, transaction history, purchase records
  • Payment Data: Virtual coin purchases and wallet transactions

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • Account Management: Creating and managing your user account
  • Social Features: Enabling chat, voice calls, and social interactions
  • Virtual Economy: Managing coin balances and gift transactions
  • Content Sharing: Facilitating music sharing and profile customization

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analytics: Understanding app usage to improve features
  • Bug Fixes: Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • Performance Optimization: Enhancing app performance and user experience

3.3 Communication

  • Push Notifications: Sending important updates and notifications
  • Customer Support: Responding to your inquiries and support requests

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: Authentication, database, and cloud services
  • Google Services: Google Sign-In and Google Play Services
  • Agora: Voice calling and real-time communication
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or government request.

4.3 Safety and Security

We may share information to protect user safety, prevent fraud, or address security threats.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: User data stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Local Storage: Music files and app preferences stored locally
  • Encryption: Data transmitted using secure protocols (HTTPS)

5.2 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Chat Messages: Stored for app functionality
  • Transaction History: Retained for financial record-keeping
  • Analytics Data: Retained for service improvement

6. Permissions We Request

6.1 Required Permissions

  • Internet Access: For app functionality and data synchronization
  • Camera: For profile photo capture
  • Microphone: For voice calls and audio features
  • Storage: For accessing and storing music files
  • Media Library: For music file selection (iOS)

6.2 Optional Permissions

  • Push Notifications: For receiving app updates and notifications

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Account Control

  • Profile Management: Update your profile information at any time
  • Account Deletion: Request account deletion through app settings
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your personal data

7.2 Privacy Settings

  • Notification Preferences: Control push notification settings
  • Privacy Controls: Manage who can see your profile and activity

7.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Analytics: Opt out of usage analytics (where available)
  • Marketing: Opt out of promotional communications

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Sending a notification through the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
